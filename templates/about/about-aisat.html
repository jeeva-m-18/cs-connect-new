About AISAT

Excellence in Education Since 2002

Our Legacy

Two decades of academic excellence and innovation

Est. 2002

A Journey of Excellence

Albertian Institute of Science and Technology (AISAT) was established in 2002 by the Bethany Educational Society, inspired by the vision of providing quality technical education to aspiring engineers. Located in the heart of Kalamassery, Kochi, AISAT has grown into one of Kerala's premier engineering institutions.

Over the past two decades, AISAT has maintained an unwavering commitment to academic excellence, research innovation, and holistic student development. Our institution has produced thousands of successful engineers who are making significant contributions in various sectors globally.

Vision & Mission

Guiding principles that drive our institution forward

Our Vision

To be a center of excellence in technical education, fostering innovation, research, and entrepreneurship to create globally competent professionals who contribute to the sustainable development of society.

Our Mission

To provide quality education with state-of-the-art infrastructure, experienced faculty, and industry collaboration. To nurture innovative thinking, ethical values, and leadership qualities in students, preparing them to meet the challenges of the global technological landscape.

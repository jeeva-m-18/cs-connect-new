Department Overview

Excellence in computing education since 2002

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering at AISAT is dedicated to fostering excellence in computing education, research, and innovation. Established in 2002, our department has consistently produced skilled engineers who excel in both industry and academia.

With state-of-the-art laboratories, experienced faculty, and strong industry collaborations, we provide a comprehensive learning environment that nurtures technical expertise, problem-solving skills, and ethical values. Our curriculum is designed to meet global standards while addressing local industry needs.

Vision & Mission

Guiding our department towards excellence

Vision

To be recognized as a premier department of Computer Science and Engineering, fostering innovation, research excellence, and producing globally competent professionals who contribute to technological advancement and societal development through ethical practices.

Mission

To provide quality education with industry-relevant curriculum, promote research and innovation, develop problem-solving and leadership skills, foster ethical values and social responsibility, and establish strong industry-academia partnerships for holistic student development.

Program Educational Objectives (PEOs)

Defining the career and professional accomplishments of our graduates

1

Professional Excellence

Graduates will be employed in professional careers in computer science and engineering or related fields, demonstrating technical competence and professional growth.

2

Problem Solving & Innovation

Graduates will analyze complex computing problems, design innovative solutions, and implement them effectively using modern tools and technologies.

3

Lifelong Learning

Graduates will engage in continuous learning, adapt to emerging technologies, and pursue advanced studies or professional development in computing and related fields.

4

Ethical & Social Responsibility

Graduates will demonstrate ethical behavior, social responsibility, and effective communication skills in their professional practice and contribute positively to society.

5

Entrepreneurship & Leadership

Graduates will exhibit entrepreneurial mindset and leadership qualities, capable of working in multidisciplinary teams and managing projects effectively.

Department Milestones

Key achievements marking our journey of excellence

2002

Department Establishment

CSE Department established with first B.Tech batch of 60 students, marking the beginning of our journey in computing education.

2008

NBA Accreditation

Received NBA accreditation, validating our commitment to quality education and continuous improvement in academic standards.

2012

Research Lab Setup

Established advanced research laboratories for IoT, AI, and Network Security, fostering innovation and research culture.
